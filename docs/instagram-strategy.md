## Overview

Instagram is the world's leading visual content platform, and for a web designer and developer, it serves as a visual portfolio, brand showcase, and audience-building channel. While it's not a direct B2B lead generation platform like LinkedIn, Instagram is powerful for showcasing visual work (web design, brand identities, project mockups) and building a brand presence that supports all other channels.

For Edmond's practice, Instagram serves as:

1. **Visual portfolio** — A scrollable gallery of completed projects, design work, and brand identities
2. **Brand presence** — A professional, active Instagram account signals legitimacy and capability to prospects who check
3. **Content distribution** — Reels and carousels reach audiences through Instagram's algorithm, independent of follower count
4. **Cross-platform hub** — Threads cross-posts feed to Instagram, creating a content multiplier effect

### 2. Content Strategy

#### Post Types and Cadence

| Post Type | Frequency | Purpose |
|-----------|-----------|---------|
| Carousel — project showcase | 1–2/month | 5–10 slides showing a completed project: hero → pages → mobile view → key features → CTA |
| Carousel — educational | 1–2/month | "5 Signs Your Business Is Losing Money to Manual Processes" — text-based carousel |
| Reel — behind the scenes | 1–2/month | Screen recording of design process, code walkthrough, or site tour |
| Reel — quick tip | 1/month | "How to check if your Google Business Profile is optimised" — 30–60 seconds |
| Story — daily/weekly | 3–5/week | Work in progress, polls, Q&A, behind-the-scenes, link shares |
| Story highlight — permanent | Ongoing | "Projects", "Services", "Free Resources", "About", "Testimonials" |

#### Carousel Strategy (Highest Engagement Format)
Carousels are Instagram's highest-engagement post format because users spend more time swiping:
- **Project showcase carousel (10 slides):**
  1. Hero shot (MockupViews desktop frame)
  2. Client brief / challenge
  3. Homepage design
  4. Mobile view
  5. Key feature #1
  6. Key feature #2
  7. Before/after comparison (if redesign)
  8. Tech stack used
  9. Client testimonial
  10. CTA — "Need a website? Book a free discovery call → link in bio"

- **Educational carousel (7–10 slides):**
  1. Hook — "5 automation workflows every SME needs"
  2–6. One workflow per slide (problem → solution → result)
  7. Summary
  8. CTA — "Save this for later · Follow for more · Free resources in bio"

#### Reel Strategy
Reels reach audiences beyond your followers through Instagram's recommendation algorithm:
- **Screen recording reels:** Record your screen showing a design process, a website audit, or a tool walkthrough. Add voice-over or text captions
- **Quick tip reels:** 30–60 second videos answering a common question: "How to check if your website is mobile-friendly" or "What is a Google Business Profile?"
- **Before/after reels:** Transition from the old site to the new site — visually dramatic and highly engaging

### 3. Stories Strategy

Stories are for engagement with existing followers — they don't reach new audiences but keep current followers warm:
- **Work in progress:** "Building a booking system for a salon today" + screenshot
- **Polls:** "Which design do you prefer — A or B?" — engagement + market research
- **Q&A:** "Ask me anything about websites and automation" — answer via video responses
- **Link shares:** Share your latest blog post, free resource, or project launch
- **Behind the scenes:** Your workspace, tools you're using, a challenge you're solving

#### Story Highlights (Permanent)
Organise Stories into Highlights on your profile:
- **Projects:** Best project showcase Stories
- **Services:** Overview of what you offer
- **Free Resources:** Links to Gumroad free downloads
- **About:** Your story, background, and credentials
- **Testimonials:** Client feedback and recommendations

### 4. Growth Strategy

#### Hashtags (3–8 per post)
Use a mix of broad and niche hashtags:
- `#webdesign` `#webdeveloper` `#botswana` `#gaborone` `#africantech` `#smallbusiness` `#websiteaudit` `#workflowautomation`

Avoid overused hashtags (`#love`, `#instagood`) — they're too competitive to be useful.

#### Engagement
- Comment genuinely on 5–10 posts per day from accounts in your niche (other developers, designers, Botswana businesses)
- Respond to every comment on your posts within the first hour (boosts algorithm visibility)
- Collaborate with complementary professionals (photographers, marketers, copywriters) — they share your work with their audience

#### Consistency Over Frequency
Posting 2–3 times per week consistently is better than posting daily for a week and then going silent for a month. Instagram's algorithm rewards accounts that maintain steady activity.

## Risks & Considerations

1. **Visual-first platform:** Instagram rewards visual content. A web developer's work (code, databases, APIs) is inherently less visual than a photographer's or designer's. **Mitigation:** Focus on the visual outputs — MockupViews mockups, screen recordings of sites, before/after comparisons, educational carousels
2. **Algorithm dependency:** Instagram's algorithm determines who sees your content. Only 10–20% of followers typically see any given post. Carousels and Reels get the highest reach
3. **Link limitation:** Only one link in bio. Use a link-in-bio tool (like a simple page on the personal website) that links to portfolio, booking, and free resources
4. **Time investment:** Creating quality carousels and Reels takes time. Batch-create content — dedicate 2–3 hours per month to creating all posts for the month, then schedule them
5. **Not a direct B2B channel:** Instagram is not where operations managers go to find web developers. It's a brand-building and credibility channel, not a lead generation channel. Set expectations accordingly

| Visual portfolio | 2–3 carousels/month | Professional gallery of completed work |

**Key insight:** Instagram is a visual credibility channel, not a primary lead generator for web design and development services. Its value lies in being a professional, active account that prospects can review — a well-maintained Instagram with project showcases, educational content, and clear service offerings signals legitimacy and capability. The content created for Instagram (carousels, Reels) can be repurposed across LinkedIn, X, and Threads, maximising the ROI on content creation time. Focus on carousels (highest engagement), maintain consistent posting (2–3/week), and use Instagram as a supporting channel to LinkedIn and the personal website.
