## 1. The Current State (Unified Audit Insights)

Based on our discovery call and my preliminary analysis of Acacia Legal Partners' digital footprint, here is the stark reality:

- **Marketing/Conversion:** Your current website is a static brochure. Your firm handles high-value corporate law, but the current intake process requires potential clients to download a PDF form and email it back.
- **Workflow / Ops:** Associates are spending an estimated 14 hours per week manually transferring intake data from emails into your CRM.
- **GEO / Search:** AI Crawlers (Google AI, ChatGPT) cannot parse your services because your site lacks an `llms.txt` file and proper `Organization` schema markup.
- **Compliance:** Your currently published privacy policy lacks an active cookie-consent banner, creating liability under data protection frameworks.

---

## 2. Proposed Digital System Architecture

To solve this, I am proposing a structural overhaul: moving away from a traditional "website" toward a "Lean-Scale" web application.

### The Foundation (Presence Tier Web Design)
- A highly-performant, 4-page Next.js web application.
- Complete Payload CMS integration allowing your clerks to post legal insights without writing code.
- Advanced Technical SEO and GA4 Analytics integrated.
- Complete visual rebrand (Typography, Colors, Iconography) reflecting top-tier legal positioning.

### The Intake Engine (Multi-Step Workflow)
- **Make.com Automation:** When a client fills the secure onboarding form on the new site, Make.com will automatically:
  1. Create a lead in HubSpot.
  2. Generate a secure, personalized Cal.com booking link for the initial consultation.
  3. Ping the managing partner via a private WhatsApp API notification.

### The Search Layer (Full Foundation GEO)
- Complete Google Business Profile optimization.
- Generative Engine Optimization (GEO) protocol implementation (FAQ Schema, `/llms.txt`) so AI assistants actively index your specific legal expertise.
