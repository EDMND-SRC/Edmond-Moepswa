# Advertising Strategy: Premium Web Systems

**Client:** Edmond Moepswa
**Target Market:** Professional Service SMEs in Botswana (Gaborone focus)
**Goal:** Book qualified Cal.com discovery calls for Web Design (P13k+) and Web Apps (P25k)

---

## 1. Funnel Architecture

**The "Anti-Agency" Funnel**
A high-trust, low-friction funnel designed for high-ticket service sales.

1. **Traffic Source:** LinkedIn Ads (Primary B2B) + Google Search (High Intent).
2. **Landing Page:** A dedicated, distraction-free version of `edmondmoepswa.com`. Heavy emphasis on the "systems vs brochures" narrative.
3. **Offer/Bribe:** "Free Architecture Review (Value P5,000)" - a scaled-down 15-minute diagnostic call instead of a hard pitch.
4. **Conversion Mechanism:** Cal.com embed directly on the landing page.

---

## 2. Budget Allocation (Recommended Monthly: P10,000)

| Platform | Budget Share | Purpose | Targeting |

| **Google Ads** | 40% (P4,000) | Capture Bottom-of-Funnel Intent | Exact match: `"web developer gaborone"`, `"custom software botswana"` |

---

## 3. Creative Hooks & Angles

### Angle 1: The "Digital Hostage" Angle (Agitation)
*Focuses on the pain of vendor lock-in, which your service specifically solves via complete handover.*
- **Hook:** "Is your web developer holding your business hostage for a P5,000/month retainer?"
- **Body:** "Most agencies build sites you can't edit without paying them. I build complete digital systems with full handover training, so you own your infrastructure."

### Angle 2: The "Expensive Brochure" Angle (Education)
*Focuses on the utility of an application vs a static site.*
- **Hook:** "A beautiful website that doesn't automate your workflow is just an expensive brochure."
- **Body:** "If your team is still managing bookings via WhatsApp and quoting via PDF, your website is failing you. Let's build a system."

---

## 4. Ad Copy Scripts

### LinkedIn Video / Thought Leadership Ad
**Format:** Talking head, casual but professional.
**Script:** 
*(First 3 Seconds)* "If you run a service business in Gaborone, your website should act like your best employee, not a static billboard."
*(Body)* "I'm Edmond. I learned systems thinking by fixing broken processes in boardrooms and hospitality. Right now, your business is leaking revenue through manual data entry, missed WhatsApp leads, and disconnected tools. I don't just 'design websites'. I deploy digital infrastructure—using tools like Make.com, Next.js, and Payload CMS—that automates your intake and scales your logic. And the best part? I train you on it and hand over the keys. No endless retainer hostage situations."
*(CTA)* "Click below to book a free 15-minute Architecture Review. Let's see what we can automate."
