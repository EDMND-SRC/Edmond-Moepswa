# SEO Local Audit

If the target domain represents a local or regional business:
- Ensure the presence of NAP (Name, Address, Phone number) in the website footer or contact pages.
- Evaluate localized schema components (via `geo-schema`).
- Suggest a strategy for Google Business Profile (GBP) posts, reviews, and Q&A management.

Generate a report formatted for `SEO-LOCAL-REPORT.md`.
