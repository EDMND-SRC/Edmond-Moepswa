# SEO Backlinks Strategy

Since enterprise tools (Ahrefs/Semrush) are not directly accessible, simulate a backlink strategy through:
- **Unlinked Brand Mentions**: Cross-reference with `geo-brands` to find mentions of the brand without a link.
- **Local Citations**: Analyze the presence of the business on generic open directories.
- **Outbound Links**: Evaluate the domain's outbound linking patterns (linking to authoritative sources).
- Ingest raw CSV data if the user directly provides a backlink export to the context.

Generate a report formatted for `SEO-BACKLINKS-REPORT.md` including a manual Link-Building Action Plan.
